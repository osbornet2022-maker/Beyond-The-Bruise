Artists’ Statement

Beyond The Bruise explores the world of Mathew Wagy, a former professional MMA fighter who has transformed his experience inside the cage into a life centered around mentorship. His gym in Deerfield Beach serves as more than a training space—it is a sanctuary where ambition is sharpened, vulnerabilities are confronted, and perseverance is built through sweat and bruises. The documentary presents Mathew not as a fighter chasing past glory, but as a coach shaping the future of the sport.

This film aims to spotlight the emotional weight behind physical discipline. The minimalist aesthetic reflects the quiet intensity of Mathew’s approach: no theatrics, no spectacle—just raw commitment. Through intimate visuals and grounded storytelling, Beyond The Bruise seeks to honor the invisible battles behind every athlete’s journey and the mentors who guide them through it.
